## 1. Introduction

ArrowGO - Arrow Puzzle ("the App") is a free mobile puzzle game provided by Mehmet Ünal. This privacy policy explains what information may be collected when you use the App, how it is used, and your rights.

By downloading or using the App, you agree to this policy.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Data stored on your device (local)

The App stores the following **only on your device**; it is not sent to our servers:

- Game progress (unlocked level, coins, stars)
- Per-level star ratings
- Sound and haptic preferences

This data is stored using `SharedPreferences` on your device.

### 2.2 Automatically collected data (third parties)

The App uses **Google AdMob** for advertising. AdMob and its partners may collect:

- Advertising identifier (IDFA / GAID)
- IP address (approximate location)
- Device model and OS version
- App usage and ad interaction data

This data is processed for ad delivery, measurement, and personalization. See [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) for details.

### 2.3 What we do NOT collect

- No account registration or email sign-up
- No access to location services
- No personal contact information
- No in-app chat or social features

## 3. How We Use Data

| Purpose | Data source |

## 4. Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent and believe your child has shared personal data, please contact us.

We do not sell your data. Sharing is limited to:
