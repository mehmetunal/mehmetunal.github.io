Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 30, 2026

ArrowGO - Arrow Puzzle ("the App") is a free mobile puzzle game developed by Mehmet Ünal. We respect your privacy.

Data we collect

  • Game progress: Unlocked level, coins, stars, and settings are stored only on your device (local storage). No account is required.
  • Ads (Google AdMob): Banner, interstitial, and optional rewarded ads are shown. The App does not use personalized ads; ad requests are sent as non-personalized (NPA). Google may still collect limited device and ad interaction data. See Google Privacy Policy.

Data we do not collect

  • No location, contacts, camera, or microphone access.
  • No personal account or email registration.
  • No personalized ads or App Tracking Transparency (ATT).

Third-party services

  • Google AdMob — advertising
  • Apple App Store / Google Play — distribution

Children

The App is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

Data deletion

You can delete game data by uninstalling the App or clearing app data in device settings.

Changes

This policy may be updated. The current version is published on this page.
