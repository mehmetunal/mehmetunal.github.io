Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 1, 2026

Smart Daily Assistant - OmniAssist (“the App”, display name: Daily Assistant) is a personal reminder and life assistant app developed by Mehmet Ünal. We respect your privacy.

Summary

The App stores your data only on your device. No data is sent to our servers, no account is required, and we do not share your data with third parties.

Data stored on your device

  • Profile: Name and language preference entered during setup.
  • Reminders & modules: Tasks, notes, alarms, vehicle maintenance, finance, health, subscriptions, shopping lists, and other module data in a local SQLite database.
  • Settings: Theme, notification preferences, and app settings in local storage (Hive).
  • PIN lock: Optional 4-digit PIN stored only as a hash on device; the PIN itself is never transmitted.
  • Backup: Exported backup files are under your control; sharing is entirely at your discretion.

Permissions

  • Notifications: To alert you for reminders and alarms (optional).
  • Biometrics: Face ID or fingerprint to unlock the app; processed by the OS, not shared with the developer.
  • Alarms / background: Platform permissions required for system-style alarms.

Data we do not collect

  • No advertising or analytics SDKs in the current version.
  • No location, contacts, camera, or microphone data.
  • No email or phone number required.
  • Data is not sold or used for ad profiling.

Third-party services

  • Apple App Store / Google Play — app distribution only

Children

The App is suitable for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

Data deletion

Uninstall the App or clear app data in device settings to remove all local data. You manage backup files separately.

Changes

This policy may be updated. The current version is published on this page.
