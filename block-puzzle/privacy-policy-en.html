Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 9, 2026

Block Grid 8x8 ("the App", bundle: com.mehmetunal.blockPuzzle) is a free mobile block puzzle game developed by Mehmet Ünal. No personal account is required.

Data we collect

  • Local game data: Score, gold, settings, difficulty preference, achievement progress, and tutorial state are stored on your device only (SharedPreferences). Not sent to our servers.
  • Ads (Google AdMob): Banner ads on menu and gameplay screens, interstitial ads at natural breaks, and optional rewarded ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data for ad delivery and measurement.
  • Tracking (iOS): App Tracking Transparency (ATT) may be requested for personalized ads. Ads are still shown if you decline.
  • GDPR / UMP: Google User Messaging Platform manages ad consent for users in applicable regions.

Data we do not collect

  • No account sign-up, email registration, or location tracking.
  • No camera, microphone, or contacts access.

Third-party services

  • Google AdMob — advertising
  • Apple App Store / Google Play — distribution

Children

The App is suitable for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

Data deletion

Delete local game data by uninstalling the App or clearing app data in device settings.

Changes

This policy may be updated. The current version is published on this page.
