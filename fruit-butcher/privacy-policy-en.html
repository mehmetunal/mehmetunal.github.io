Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 15, 2026

Fruit Butcher (“the app”, bundle: com.mehmetunal.fruitButcher / com.mehmetunal.fruit_butcher) is a free mobile arcade game developed by Mehmet Ünal. No personal account is required.

Data we collect

  • Local game data: Scores, settings, story progress, coins, quests, and achievements are stored only on your device (Hive / SharedPreferences). Nothing is sent to our servers.
  • Ads (Google AdMob): Interstitial, rewarded, and optional app open ads are shown. No banner ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data for ad delivery and measurement.
  • Tracking permission (iOS): App Tracking Transparency (ATT) may be requested for personalized ads.
  • GDPR / UMP: Google User Messaging Platform manages ad consent for users in applicable regions.
  • Vibration permission (Android / iOS): Used solely for haptic feedback.

Data we do not collect

  • No account creation, email registration, or location tracking.
  • No access to camera, microphone, or contacts.

Third-party services

  • Google AdMob — ad delivery
  • Apple App Store / Google Play — app distribution

Children

The app is suitable for a general audience (PEGI 3 / Everyone). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

Data deletion

Uninstall the app or clear app data in device settings to remove local game data.

Changes

This policy may be updated. The current version is published on this page.
