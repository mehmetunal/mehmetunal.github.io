Privacy Policy — Let the Number Fall

Last updated: June 30, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Mehmet UNAL ("we", "us") handles information when you use the mobile game Let the Number Fall ("the App").

1. Information We Collect

The App may collect and process the following types of data through third-party services:

  • Device identifiers — used for advertising and analytics (via Google AdMob).
  • Advertising data — ad interactions, impressions, and related metrics.
  • Usage data — general app usage to improve ad delivery and performance.
  • Game progress stored locally — high score and gold balance are saved on your device using local storage and are not transmitted to our servers.

2. How We Use Information

  • To display advertisements (rewarded and interstitial ads).
  • To measure ad performance and prevent fraud.
  • To save your game progress locally on your device.

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses Google AdMob to show ads. AdMob may collect data according to Google's policies:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • AdMob & Privacy

You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings (iOS: Settings → Privacy → Apple Advertising).

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Data may be shared with advertising partners (Google) solely to provide ads and measure their performance.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Data Retention

Local game data remains on your device until you uninstall the App or clear app data. Third-party ad partners retain data according to their own policies.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your region, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal data. Contact us using the email below.

8. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the App after changes means you accept the updated policy.
