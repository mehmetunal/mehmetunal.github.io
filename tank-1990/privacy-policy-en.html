Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 14, 2026

Tank 1990 Arcade ("the App", bundle: com.tank1990.tank1990) is a free mobile arcade tank game developed by Mehmet Ünal. No personal account is required.

Data we collect

  • Local game data: high score, sound / difficulty settings, language preference, unlocked stages, achievements, daily quests, gold, and shop inventory are stored on your device only (SharedPreferences). Not sent to our servers.
  • Ads (Google AdMob): Banner, interstitial, and optional rewarded ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data for ad delivery and measurement.
  • Tracking (iOS): App Tracking Transparency (ATT) may be requested for personalized ads.
  • GDPR / UMP: Google User Messaging Platform manages ad consent where required.

Data we do not collect

  • No account sign-up, email registration, or location tracking.
  • No camera, microphone, or contacts access.

Third-party services

  • Google AdMob — advertising
  • Apple App Store / Google Play — distribution

Children

The App is suitable for a general audience (Everyone / PEGI 7 guidance — mild fantasy violence). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. Ad creatives follow Google policies.

Data deletion

Delete local game data by uninstalling the App or clearing app data in device settings. The in-app “Reset progress” option also clears local data.

Changes

This policy may be updated. The current version is published on this page.
