Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 16, 2026

Mind Labyrinth (Turkish: Zihin Labirenti; Android: com.zihinlabirenti.zihin_labirenti, iOS: com.zihinlabirenti.zihinLabirenti) is a free mobile puzzle game developed by Mehmet Ünal. It runs fully offline and does not require a personal account.

Data we collect

The App does not collect personal identity information and does not use ad or analytics SDKs.

  • Local game data: Progress (levels, stars, XP, achievements, memory shards) and settings (theme, language, sound, music, notifications, accessibility) are stored on your device only (SQLite and SharedPreferences). Nothing is sent to our servers.
  • Local notifications (optional): Daily streak reminders are scheduled on-device; notification content is not sent to a server.

Backup

JSON backups created from Settings remain under your control. Sharing uses the system share sheet; files are not uploaded to our servers (there are none).

Data we do not collect

  • No account sign-up, email registration, or location tracking.
  • No camera, microphone, or contacts access.
  • No advertising or third-party analytics SDKs.

Third-party services

  • Apple App Store / Google Play — distribution

Children

The App is aimed at a general audience and has no accounts or online chat. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

Data deletion

Delete local game data by uninstalling the App or clearing app data in device settings.

Changes

This policy may be updated. The current version is published on this page.
